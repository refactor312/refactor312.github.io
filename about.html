About

Our Mission is to provide charitable support to under-resourced nonprofit organizations by delivering pro bono data science, analytics, and technology consulting services through skilled volunteers seeking educational growth and hands-on experience. Through this collaborative approach, we aim to enhance nonprofit capacity and reach, strengthen community impact, and foster experiential learning and career development within Chicago and beyond.

What We Do

Analytics

Dashboarding, market research, KPI tracking

Data Engineering

Dataset preparation, ETL pipelines

AI Consultation

Tech communication for STEM teams, responsible AI in industry

Cloud Modernization

ML solutions, dataset migration

Our Story

We started Refactor 312 as four astrophysicists who wanted to use our technical skills for direct community impact. After grad school, we saw two things: nonprofits across Chicago doing powerful work without access to affordable data or technology support, and young technologists eager to gain experience in a challenging job market.

We began by helping local organizations with data science and coding, and it quickly grew into something bigger. We wanted to prove that technology doesn’t have to feel distant or corporate, but can be personal, community-driven, and do good.
