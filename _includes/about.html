About Us

    Our mission is to create technological solutions that support charitable organizations and initiatives through advanced technology with an emphasis on community impact in Chicago. We leverage these initiatives as opportunities to educate and offer valuable experience to emerging technologists, early-career professionals, and career changers.

  • Drive Social Change & Community Impact

    Empowering nonprofits to make a lasting difference by fostering positive social and environmental change.

  • Elevate Emerging Tech Talent

    Offering a platform for rising stars in technology to showcase their skills and make an impact through meaningful projects.

  • Advance Technology for Good

    Leverging innovative solutions to amplify impact, streamline operations, and create sustainable futures.
